Neighbors,

Thank you for visiting my website.  I have been a resident of Grandview since 2006 and was a member of the Planning Commission and Tax Increment Finance Commission from 2009 to 2013.  I was reelected on April 2nd, 2013 and have served on the Board of Alderman since January of 2013.

I am passionate about continually improving the quality of life for our citizens.  Since taking office I have been focused on transportation and infrastructure, redevelopment, and revitalization of our park amenities. I believe that together we can build a better Grandview.

Please feel free to e-mail me with any questions or suggestions you may have on improving our community.

Most Sincerely,
