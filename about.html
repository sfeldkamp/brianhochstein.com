Brian Hochstein was born in Wayne, NE.

He attended Kansas State University earning a degree in Landscape Architecture in 2004.

Following college and a job related move from Wichita, KS, Brian and his wife Anne decided to make Grandview their home in 2006.

Professionally Brian practices as a Landscape Architect in Missouri, Kansas, and Iowa.  He is a member of the American Society of Landscape Architects and has previously served as Treasurer for the local chapter.

Brian served as a member of the Grandview Planning Commission and Tax Increment Finance Commission from 2009 - 2013.

He received his Master of Business degree from the University of Phoenix in 2013.  

Brian and his wife Anne live in Ward II with their three children.  He is a member of St. Thomas More Catholic Church and the Knights of Columbus.
